# eKaratig
 
**eKaratig** is a Malolos City-wide digital service for requesting, tracking, and managing government documents — built to make dealing with local government paperwork faster, more transparent, and less of a hassle.
 
> "Karatig" reflects the idea of being *near* and *connected* — bringing city services closer to residents, wherever they are.
 
## Overview
 
eKaratig lets Malolos City residents:
 
- **Request government documents** online instead of lining up at city hall
- **Track the status** of applications in real time — from submission to release
- **View official city news and announcements** as they're published
- **Store issued documents in a digital wallet**, which acts as verifiable proof of the documents they've chosen to keep on the app
The goal is to reduce the friction of getting basic civil and government documents processed, while giving the city a modern, centralized channel for public communication.
 
## Core Features
 
### 📄 Document Applications
Residents can submit requests for various government documents directly through the app, with clear status tracking (in progress, completed/released) so they always know where their request stands.
 
### 🗂️ Digital Document Wallet
Once a document is issued, it can be added to the user's wallet inside the app — serving as a portable, easy-to-access proof of the document without needing to carry a physical copy everywhere.
 
### 📰 City News & Announcements
Official announcements, advisories, and updates from the Malolos city government are posted directly in-app, sorted by **Latest**, **Popular**, or **Oldest**, so residents stay informed without needing to check multiple sources.
 
### 🔔 Status Transparency
A clear status legend helps residents understand exactly where each of their applications is in the process — reducing uncertainty and repeat inquiries to city offices.
